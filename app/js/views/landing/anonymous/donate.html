Donate food

You own or work in a business that sometimes has surplus food at the end of the day? You don’t like seeing good food going to waste? Whether it is a restaurant, a catering company, a supermarket, a food store, a stall etc, whether it is big or small, operating on a regular basis or occasional — you can make a difference! It is simple and easy and you will make so many people happy.

Simply register as a business on our website using the link below. And if you know of any business that might like to donate surplus food, please spread the word - the more the merrier!

Donate money

There are so many great charities asking for your financial support — what makes Plan Zheroes different is that we support many of those charities too! By offering them free sources of good quality food, they are saving money and can use more of their budgets to provide their service. It’s a real win-win! With your financial support we can recruit more people to get food to the people who really need it.

Please be as generous as you can and rest assured that every penny of your donation will be used efficiently for our cause.
