Our Team

Laura Hopper

CEO

Laura joined Plan Zheroes as CEO on 1st October 2015. Laura comes to Plan Zheroes from the homelessness sector where she was developing learning, training and employability programmes for homeless people, including a food growing project and a bakery social enterprise. She witnessed first-hand how food can transform peoples' lives and is passionate about the issue of food poverty. Laura also co-founded and operated a café/coffee roasting business operating in Warsaw, Poland and previously worked as an equity research analyst for several international banks. She has an MBA from Columbia University in New York and a BA in Economics from Princeton University.

Jack Fargher

Technology & Community Manager

Having worked in customer relations at an artisan bakery for the last 3 and half years, Jack’s skills of research, training and analysis were instrumental in improving operational requirements and customer satisfaction, cultivating close networks with internal departments and customers to facilitate and instigate changes as the bakery undertook phenomenal growth. Having studied History at university and being a passionate home cook, he’s also keen researcher and reader of absolutely anything to do with food, culture and history. As Technology and Community Manager, Jack works with our partners to ensure that our platform is user friendly and efficient.

Our board and trustees

Shane Holland

Trustee and Chief Zero Food Waste Hero

Shane has extensive experience within the third sector, in particular within food, sustainability and working with people with poor life chances. He is the Director for England of Slow Food UK, setting up their schools’ food education programme and their political lobbying division, as well as being involved with a host of prison and school projects across London and the UK. His professional background in food operations brings us knowledge of what food businesses require and bridges this with the needs of our charity partners. He is a frequent public speaker on food, sustainability, and the impact food poverty has on individuals. He is a Fellow of the Chartered Management Institute and the RSA. Shane was Interim CEO of Plan Zheroes from August-September 2015.

Andrew Mercieca

Finance Trustee

Andrew brings his knowledge of PZ and his experience as one of our founding trustees to the fore. Andrew has a wealth of experience as a finance director in major global businesses, within the technology and pharmaceutical sectors. He is a Chartered Accountant and played a critical role in registering Plan Zheroes as a CIO (Charitable Incorporated Organisation) in 2013. His sound judgment, knowledge of good governance and financial expertise help PZ make informed financial decisions and ensures that we satisfy compliance related matters including charity commission reporting, internal controls, governance and insurance.

Carla Morris Papps

Trustee and Navigator of the Legal Jungle

Carla founded Cotswold Barristers as a dedicated Direct Public Access Barristers' Chambers to provide elite legal services to businesses and individuals on property and commercial law. Her experiences in property investment and in the management of chambers guided her to examine and implement ways that legal services could be provided by focussing on direct public access to talent and specialism within the Bar. Within three months of founding Cotswold Barristers, Carla oversaw the management and client care of over 250 clients in the biggest single direct access case in UK history. Carla's invaluable knowledge helps PZ by providing legal perspective and a young managerial mindset.

Phil Hill

Trustee and Digital Explorer

Phil is as an authority inside the world of new technologies, innovations and start-ups. He is a highly experienced consultant, specialising in interesting and innovative IT and Operations projects. A thought leader and "game changer", he builds on 35 years of work with London Insurance Market as CIO and Operations Director. He now provides high level management advice to startups, small companies and business units on how to meet the regulatory, registration, and audit needs of their market. This is further augmented by business development services that help small organisations succeed through the pains of growth or change.
