How We Work

Plan Zheroes is a social network, where relationships are built between food businesses and charities, communication is quick and simple and food reaches those in need, safely and conveniently.

1
When a business has surplus food to donate, they upload their information online
2
Nearby charities receive a notification of the available food and can claim the food online
3
Volunteers and transporters in the local area get involved by helping transport the food

What you need to know

Food donations are legal

Well... contrary to what most people believe donations of surplus food are perfectly legal in the UK! Depending on the type of food to be donated a few requirements need to be in place such as appropriate storage, temperature, packing and how to handle food. Charities and businesses sign an agreement which confirms the charity takes full responsibility for the safety of the food once they receive it and the business only donates food that is fit for human consumption.

Zheroes near you

Use the Zheroes map to find that bakery around the corner with lots of tasty bread to donate at 6pm, and that amazing community project down the street helping a group of young people living in food poverty. Then discover those volunteers and transporters who are so keen to help when it is difficult for a charity to collect the food available to them.

Build your Community

Using our Food Map, you can search and filter for exactly the types of food and donation sizes that meet your requirements/specific criteria, as well as search for specific types of Zheroes. With this, you can build your community so that whenever you have food for donation or help receiving this donation, those who can help will know straight away.
