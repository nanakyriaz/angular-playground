AGREEMENT:

1. Background

It is important to Plan Zheroes that good food does not go to waste, so we inspire food donors (food retailers, caterers, restaurants, hotels etc.) to donate food that would otherwise be thrown away at the end of the day to reputable charities. The food is donated so that the charities may distribute it to those in genuine need. This agreement clarifies the conditions Plan Zheroes makes in linking donors to charities.
